- Excerpt -

They would continue on. In each other's eyes, they were the one who shattered the darkness, never to part again. Colors bled into the monochrome, that vivid, pulsating, dazzling hue, clearly heralding their unswerving affection. Seeing both the past and present, They hold the courage to embrace the undulating journey ahead.

Together,

each becomes the savior of the other.

The fireworks beautifully concealed them—now, as ever, fireworks form their backdrop—a splendid pursuit if one could ignore the various magical shouts and laughter.

Stupefy.

Expelliarmus.

Protego.

“Patronus can change, transforming during times of great turmoil and emotional upheaval.”

“...Expecto Patronum.”

A stag leaped from the tip of Draco's wand, a silver creature that circled him twice before joyously bounding into the kingdom of snow, carrying the boy’s heart through the grey clouds to meet the hidden winter sun beyond.
